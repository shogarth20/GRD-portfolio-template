As a Designer 

Sarah is an up incoming graduate for the graphic design program at St. Clair College, but that hasn't stopped her from dipping her toes into the industry. She currently works remotely at Sparks Marketing Communications, located in London Ontario, where she works with a creative director that guides her to do her best. She created a plethora of vital components to a thriving business such as ads, social posts, brand guidelines, Business cards, 3D mock ups, website images and icons. When she's not working to her overall goal of working in the film and design industry, you can find her watching some obscure movie, creating a playlist that expresses any emotion the human body is capable of, drawing whatever has caught her interest this week or hanging out with friends and family.

ABOUT ME

My name is Sarah Hogarth, and I am in my final year of the graphic design program at St. Clair College, while simultaneously working part time at a design agency. I’ve grown up in the industry, and always had an interest in design. Ever since I can remember I've been creative. Always drawing or taking pictures. In my formative years I started getting into film analysis and production, soon realizing I could pursue a career that encompassed all these aspects. During my time at St. Clair, I’ve learned a lot of useful skills, not only in design but in ways I express myself. I have gained so much confidence, not only in myself, but in my work. I use the skills I've learned in my junior designer role. I create ads, social posts, brand guidelines, Business cards, 3D mock ups, website images and icons and much more.
